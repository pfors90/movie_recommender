Welcome to "What Should I Watch?," a movie recommendation ending build in Python + Flask, powered by scikit learn. This webapp is intended to help you find the next movie to watch during your marathon. While streaming services do offer tailored recommendations, not everyone is locked to a single platform, and websites like IMDb tend to leave much to be desired by their "Related Movies" sections.

To proceed, click on "What Should I Watch?" above and enter up to five movies (preferably at least somewhat related for the best success), and press submit! The app will analyze the keywords that your selected movies have been tagged with and compare against a database of around 20k potential matches for the five movies that you'd probably like to watch next.
